About Telve Digital

Telve Digital is not just an independent music label; it is a vision for the future of music. Built on the Proton Radio platform, Telve Digital combines the artistry of music with cutting-edge technology to create a transparent and empowering ecosystem for musicians and creators.

While we excel as a label by fostering trust and collaboration, our ambitions go beyond that. Telve Digital is also a technology company, striving to redefine how music is created, shared, and enjoyed. In the near future, we aim to launch a groundbreaking music platform with advanced features, offering new ways for artists and fans to connect.

How It Works:

  • Statements: Royalties are meticulously tracked and sent once your balance reaches $100 or more.
  • Payments: Payments are made quarterly, ensuring regular and predictable income.
  • Schedule: Statements and payments are processed two months after the end of each quarter:
    • Q1 (Jan-Mar): Statements by June 1st
    • Q2 (Apr-Jun): Statements by September 1st
    • Q3 (Jul-Sep): Statements by December 1st
    • Q4 (Oct-Dec): Statements by March 1st
  • Transparency: Artists can view detailed earnings reports, and any withholdings are carried over to the next quarter for easy tracking.

Platforms like Beatport pay distributors one month after each quarter ends, and Proton handles all statement generation shortly thereafter. This ensures that every transaction is clear and accounted for.

At Telve Digital, we believe transparency builds trust. By partnering with Proton Radio, we empower artists to focus on their creativity while we ensure their work is fairly recognized and compensated.
